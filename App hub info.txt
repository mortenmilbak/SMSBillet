Buy your ticket for bus, train and metro in the greater Copenhagen area by sms. This application is in Danish and does only work in Denmark.
--------
Køb din billet til bus, tog og metro i Københavns-området via SMS. Denne applikation hjælper dig med at danne den sms du skal sende for at købe din smsbillet.
